What is this page?

Someone you know wants to help you get some information. While sending you the information directly would potentially them seem helpful, they know that the most helpful thing they could do is to encourage informational empowerment through searching the internet.

Is there something wrong with asking for help?

NO, there is absolutely nothing wrong with asking for help if you need it. However, unless we have tried and failed to help ourselves, we don't know if the help is necessary.

In fact, because we become better at activities the more we do them, the best way to learn to search effectively is repeated practice.
